# AI UGC Marketing Campaign Summary

## Project Overview

This project demonstrates how Prompt Engineering and Artificial Intelligence can be used to generate authentic User-Generated Content (UGC) advertisements for local businesses.

The campaign was designed for **Brew Buzz Cafe**, a modern café located in **Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh**, with the objective of increasing brand awareness, customer engagement, and in-store visits through short-form social media content.

---

# Campaign Objective

- Increase local brand awareness
- Improve customer engagement
- Generate authentic UGC advertisements
- Drive more café visits
- Build customer trust

---

# Target Audience

- College Students
- Working Professionals
- Coffee Enthusiasts
- Families
- Young Adults (18–35)
