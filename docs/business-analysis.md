# Business Analysis

## Business Overview

Brew Buzz Cafe is a customer-focused café that combines quality beverages with a welcoming atmosphere. It primarily serves students, professionals, and coffee lovers seeking a comfortable place to relax, work, or meet friends.

---

## Current Marketing Challenges

- Limited online visibility
- Low social media engagement
- Lack of consistent promotional content
- Limited customer-generated content
- Increasing competition from nearby cafés

---

## Opportunities

- Growing popularity of Instagram Reels
- High engagement through authentic UGC videos
- Strong local community presence
- AI-powered content creation for consistent marketing

---

## Competitor Analysis

Nearby cafés actively use short-form videos and influencer-style content to attract younger audiences. Brew Buzz Cafe can compete by adopting authentic UGC marketing strategies that showcase customer experiences instead of traditional advertisements.

---

## Business Goals

- Increase brand awareness
- Improve customer engagement
- Increase café footfall
- Build customer trust
- Strengthen online presence

---

## Expected Benefits

Implementing AI-generated UGC advertisements can help Brew Buzz Cafe maintain a consistent marketing strategy while reducing content creation time and increasing audience engagement.
