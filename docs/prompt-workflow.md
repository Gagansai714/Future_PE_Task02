# Prompt Engineering Workflow

## Workflow Overview

The project follows a structured Prompt Engineering approach to create authentic User Generated Content (UGC) advertisements for local businesses.

---

## Workflow

Business Research

↓

Audience Analysis

↓

Marketing Goal Definition

↓

Prompt Design

↓

Hook Generation

↓

UGC Script Creation

↓

Platform Adaptation

↓

CTA Generation

↓

Caption Generation

↓

Content Review

↓

Marketing-Ready Content

---

## Workflow Explanation

### Business Research

Understand the client's business, services, target audience, and marketing objectives.

### Audience Analysis

Identify customer demographics, interests, pain points, and preferred social media platforms.

### Prompt Design

Develop reusable AI prompts that consistently generate high-quality UGC content.

### AI Content Generation

Generate hooks, scripts, CTAs, captions, and platform-specific content.

### Content Review

Refine AI-generated outputs to ensure authenticity, readability, and marketing effectiveness.

### Final Output

Deliver professional UGC advertisement content ready for publishing across multiple social media platforms.
