# Client Brief

## Project

AI UGC Ad Content Generator for Local Businesses

---

## Client Information

**Business Name:** Brew Buzz Cafe

**Industry:** Cafe & Coffee Shop

**Location:** Visakhapatnam (Vizag), Andhra Pradesh, India

---

## Business Overview

Brew Buzz Cafe is a modern café that offers handcrafted coffee, refreshing beverages, quick snacks, and a comfortable environment for students, professionals, families, and coffee enthusiasts. The café aims to provide a relaxing space where customers can work, socialize, and enjoy premium-quality food and beverages.

---

## Project Objective

Design an AI-powered Prompt Engineering system capable of generating authentic User Generated Content (UGC) advertisements for social media platforms. The generated content should increase brand awareness, improve customer engagement, and encourage more café visits.

---

## Target Audience

- College Students
- Working Professionals
- Coffee Enthusiasts
- Friends & Families
- Young Adults (18–35 Years)

---

## Marketing Platforms

- Instagram Reels
- Facebook Reels
- YouTube Shorts
- TikTok (Conceptual)

---

## Deliverables

- UGC Hooks
- Short Ad Scripts
- Platform-Specific Content
- Call-to-Action Library
- Instagram Captions
- Prompt Engineering Documentation

---

## Expected Outcome

Develop a reusable Prompt Engineering workflow capable of generating high-quality, platform-ready UGC advertisements that can be adapted for multiple businesses.
